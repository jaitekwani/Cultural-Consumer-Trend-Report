Methodology

Each trend is scored 0–100 from a weighted signal table (source citation, signal type, datapoint, and a per-signal weight); composite scores above 75 are tier'd Hot, 55–74 Rising, and 40–54 Watch. Every trend is logged once, in one issue, under one of five fixed categories — Fashion & Style, Wellness & Health, Food & Drink, Lifestyle & Culture, and Alcohol. This is a simulated weekly intelligence exercise: source citations are illustrative and attributed to real-sounding institutions for format realism, and should not be treated as live research.
